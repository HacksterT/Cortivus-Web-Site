Journey2Health

Digital Health Assistant for Chronic Disease Management

Journey2Health consolidates patient medical records into simplified health profiles and uses AI agents to support chronic disease self-management. The platform helps patients understand their conditions, track symptoms, and make informed decisions about their care.

Key Features:

  • Medical record consolidation and simplification
  • AI-powered health insights and recommendations
  • Symptom tracking and pattern recognition
  • Personalized care plan assistance
  • Integration with healthcare provider systems

Target Users: Patients with chronic conditions (diabetes, hypertension, heart disease)

Status: MVP in development, pilot testing Q4 2025

Technology: Python, AI/ML, Healthcare APIs, HIPAA-compliant infrastructure

Our Product Development Philosophy

At Cortivus, we believe AI should solve real problems, not create new ones. Each of our products starts with a genuine need we've identified through our experience in healthcare, technology, and working with diverse communities.

We build products that are:

  • User-Centered: Designed with real users and their actual workflows in mind
  • Technically Sound: Built on proven AI/ML technologies with robust infrastructure
  • Ethically Responsible: Respecting privacy, security, and human agency
  • Commercially Viable: Sustainable business models that benefit users and creators

Our diverse product portfolio demonstrates our ability to apply AI across different domains while maintaining high standards for quality, usability, and impact.
