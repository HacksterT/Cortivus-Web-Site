Questions

Frequently Asked

What's the difference between the tiers?

All tiers include full platform access. Founding Members get early adopter pricing ($99 for 6 months, then $199 in perpetuity) and direct input on features. First CMO pricing is for executives new to the C-suite. Platform is standard pricing.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. All subscriptions are month-to-month with no long-term commitment. Cancel anytime and retain access through the end of your billing period.

What about the Premium Advisory?

The Mentor includes an optional Premium Advisory tier with direct 1:1 access to our founder. Contact us to discuss availability and pricing.

Is my data private?

We never sell your information. Your Career Profile powers only your own recommendations—not shared across users or used for advertising.
