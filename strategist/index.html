A research partner, not a chatbot

The coach reads your organization’s profile before it says anything, so the advice is about your budget, your county, and your mandates. It never proposes sharing information about the people you serve.

It researches your ecosystem

Point it at your region and it searches the live web and X for the funders, agencies, health systems, and peer nonprofits around you. Every suggestion comes back as a draft card with its sources, and nothing joins your map until you keep it.

It ranks what to do first

Your map crossed with your own numbers: funding concentration, unrestricted share, months of reserve. The result is a short list of relationship moves, each one naming the fact about your organization that drives it.

It thinks it through with you

Ask why a move comes first and you get a plain-language case you can argue with, then carry straight into your annual cycle as a strategic issue. It coaches; you decide.
