Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Last updated: June 11, 2026

1. Who We Are

Cortivus is a clinical AI safety consulting firm operated by Troy Sybert as a sole proprietorship. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal information collected through the cortivus.com website and in connection with consulting engagements.

Contact: servingyou@cortivus.com

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Contact form submissions — name, email address, organization, and message content submitted through the website contact form
  • Mailing list signups — email address provided to receive the Cortivus Insights mailing list
  • Engagement information — during a consulting engagement, information you share about your organization, AI systems, clinical workflows, vendor relationships, and operational context
  • Billing information — name, organization, and contact details provided for invoicing purposes

Information collected automatically

  • Basic server and security logs — IP address, browser type, and request timestamps collected by Cloudflare as part of website delivery and security
  • Bot protection interactions — interaction data collected by Cloudflare Turnstile on the contact form to distinguish human visitors from automated traffic

Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not use analytics or behavioral tracking tools (no Google Analytics, no pixel trackers)
  • We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking purposes
  • We do not collect data about you from third-party sources
  • We do not operate a user account system on this website

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Respond to inquiries — Follow up on contact form submissions to discuss potential engagements
  • Deliver consulting services — Conduct assessments, prepare deliverables, and communicate throughout the engagement
  • Send the Insights mailing list — Deliver new briefings, tracker updates, and regulatory summaries to subscribers who have opted in
  • Process invoices and payments — Manage billing for consulting engagements

We do not use your information for:

  • Advertising or marketing profiling
  • Selling or renting to third parties
  • Training AI models

4. Third-Party Services

We use a limited number of third-party services to operate the website. Each processes only the minimum data necessary for its function:

Service Purpose Data Shared
Cloudflare Website hosting, CDN, security, and bot protection IP address, request metadata, Turnstile interaction data
Stripe Credit card payment processing for consulting invoices Name, email, payment details, billing address

Engagement information shared during a consulting engagement is handled directly by Cortivus and is not shared with third parties except as required to deliver the engagement or as required by law.

5. Data Security

All connections to cortivus.com are encrypted via HTTPS, terminated at Cloudflare's edge network. Contact form submissions are transmitted securely to our API endpoint.

Engagement documents and correspondence are handled via professional communication channels. While we implement reasonable security precautions, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of information transmitted electronically.

6. Data Retention

  • Contact form submissions — Retained as long as necessary to respond and to maintain a record of the inquiry relationship
  • Mailing list — Retained until you unsubscribe; you may unsubscribe at any time by replying to any mailing list email
  • Engagement records — Retained in accordance with standard professional record-keeping practices and any obligations specified in the engagement agreement
  • Server logs — Basic Cloudflare logs are retained per Cloudflare's standard retention schedule

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — Request information about what personal data we hold about you
  • Correct your data — Request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Delete your data — Request deletion of your personal information, subject to any legal or contractual retention obligations
  • Unsubscribe — Opt out of the mailing list at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at servingyou@cortivus.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended exclusively for healthcare organizations and working professionals. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18.

9. International Users

Cortivus is operated from the United States. If you access the website or engage our services from outside the United States, your information will be processed in the United States. By using the website or engaging our services, you consent to this processing.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted to this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
