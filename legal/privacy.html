Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Last updated: February 11, 2026

1. Who We Are

Cortivus is an AI-powered career intelligence platform for healthcare executives, operated by Troy Sybert as a sole proprietorship. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information when you use the Cortivus Platform at app.cortivus.com and our website at cortivus.com.

Contact: servingyou@cortivus.com

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Account information — email address and password (managed by Supabase Auth)
  • Profile information — name, professional title, industry, organization, and other career details you enter
  • Career content — resumes, cover letters, professional history, skills, certifications, education, and other documents you upload or create
  • Platform interactions — goals, objectives, check-ins, AI coaching conversations, interview practice sessions, and strategic plans you create within the Platform
  • Contact form submissions — name, email, and message content when you contact us through the website

Information collected automatically

  • Authentication data — login timestamps, session tokens, and account activity (managed by Supabase)
  • Basic server logs — IP address, browser type, and request timestamps for security and error monitoring

Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools (no Google Analytics, no pixel trackers)
  • We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking purposes
  • We do not collect data from third-party sources about you

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide the Platform — Process your career data through AI to generate personalized recommendations, coaching, and career tools
  • Maintain your account — Authenticate you, manage your subscription, and communicate about your account
  • Improve the Platform — Fix bugs, resolve errors, and improve functionality (using aggregated, non-identifying data)
  • Send transactional emails — Account verification, password reset, and subscription notifications

We do not use your data for:

  • Training AI models for other users
  • Advertising or marketing profiling
  • Selling or renting to third parties
  • Sharing across user accounts

4. Third-Party Services

We use a limited number of third-party services to operate the Platform. Each processes only the minimum data necessary for its function:

Service Purpose Data Shared
Supabase Authentication Email, password (hashed), login activity
Paddle Payment processing Email, payment details, billing address
Resend Transactional email delivery Email address (for sending verification and reset emails)
Cloudflare Network security, SSL, DNS IP address, request metadata (in transit)
DigitalOcean Database hosting All application data (stored encrypted at rest)

We do not share your career content (resumes, goals, coaching sessions, etc.) with any third party. AI processing happens on our own infrastructure.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a managed PostgreSQL database hosted by DigitalOcean with:

  • Encryption at rest and in transit (SSL/TLS)
  • Automated daily backups
  • Access restricted to the application server via trusted sources

All connections between you and the Platform are encrypted via HTTPS, terminated at Cloudflare's edge network.

While we implement reasonable security measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

6. Data Retention

  • Active accounts — Your data is retained for as long as your account is active
  • Deleted accounts — All personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Server logs — Basic server logs are retained for up to 90 days for security monitoring, then deleted
  • Backups — Database backups containing your data are automatically rotated and deleted per DigitalOcean's retention schedule

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — View all personal information we hold about you through the Platform
  • Export your data — Request a copy of your data by contacting us
  • Correct your data — Update your information through the Platform at any time
  • Delete your data — Delete your account through the Platform's settings, which permanently removes all your data
  • Withdraw consent — Stop using the Platform at any time by cancelling your subscription and deleting your account

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at servingyou@cortivus.com.

8. Children's Privacy

The Platform is designed for working professionals and is not intended for anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. International Users

The Platform is operated from the United States. If you access the Platform from outside the United States, your data will be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using the Platform, you consent to this transfer.

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, Paddle (as Merchant of Record) handles payment data in compliance with GDPR. For questions about your rights under GDPR, contact us at servingyou@cortivus.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you via email or a notice on the Platform at least 30 days before the changes take effect. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

Contact
