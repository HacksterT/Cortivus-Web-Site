Refund Policy

Billing terms for Cortivus consulting engagements.

Last updated: June 11, 2026

Consulting Fees Are Non-Refundable

Cortivus provides professional consulting services. Fees invoiced for work completed are non-refundable. By engaging Cortivus and authorizing commencement of work, you acknowledge and accept this policy.

This applies to all engagement types, including SAFE AI assessments, Quick Read engagements, and any other scoped consulting services.

No Guarantee of Results

Consulting deliverables represent professional analysis based on information provided and conditions observed at the time of the engagement. Cortivus does not guarantee specific outcomes, risk reductions, regulatory approvals, or the prevention of AI-related adverse events. Dissatisfaction with outcomes does not constitute grounds for a refund.

Engagement Cancellation

Either party may cancel a consulting engagement in accordance with the terms of the engagement agreement. Upon cancellation:

  • You are responsible for payment of all fees for work completed through the cancellation date
  • Deliverables completed and delivered prior to cancellation are yours to retain, subject to full payment of fees owed
  • Fees paid in advance for work not yet commenced may be applied to a future engagement at Cortivus's discretion

Billing Errors

If you believe an invoice contains an error — such as a duplicate charge or an amount inconsistent with the agreed engagement terms — contact us promptly at servingyou@cortivus.com. Documented billing errors will be corrected and any overpayment will be refunded.

Pre-Engagement Consultations

Initial consultations and discovery calls held prior to a formal engagement agreement are provided at no charge and are not subject to this policy.

Contact

For billing questions or to report a billing error, contact us at servingyou@cortivus.com.
