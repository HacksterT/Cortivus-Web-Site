The Framework

Anatomy of a Mentor Cycle

13 weeks of structured growth and accountability.

1

PI Planning

Week 1: Define your strategic objectives for the quarter. What do you want to accomplish? What does success look like?

2

Sprint Execution

Weeks 2-12: Six two-week sprints with clear goals. Regular check-ins keep you accountable and moving forward.

3

Retrospective

Week 13: Review outcomes, document achievements, and prepare for your next cycle. What worked? What's next?
