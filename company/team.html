Our Mission & Values

Mission

To bring twenty years of patient safety discipline to the AI systems entering clinical care, so hospitals can deploy AI without inheriting unmanaged risk.

Core Values

  • Clinical Discipline First: AI safety belongs inside the governance structures your hospital already runs
  • Patient Safety at the Center: Every framework we apply traces back to whether care is safer with this AI than without
  • Independent Assessment: Our value depends on telling you what your vendor will not

Why We Do This

Healthcare leaders are being asked to deploy AI into clinical workflows faster than governance can keep up. Vendors will not show you their failure modes. Your quality committee speaks RCA and FMEA, but no one is applying those frameworks to the AI being deployed into care. SAFE AI exists because the discipline already exists. It just needs to be applied to a new class of system.
